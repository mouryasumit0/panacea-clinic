Dr. Sameer Deepak Lote

MBBS, MD, DNB, MNAMS, EDARM, FAPSR, Fellowship in Pulmonology from CMC, Vellore

Dr. Sameer Lote has a vast experience of over more than 10 years in his field of pulmonology. He has gloriously completed MBBS from NKP Salve medical college in Nagpur with honors in ENT and a top ranker in third MBBS Part I and II (2008-13)

He secured his dream branch in the very first attempt and pursued MD in respiratory medicine from Pramukhswami medical college - Shri Krishna hospital, Anand, Gujarat. He passed MD with highest recorded score ever in the department of pulmonology and was a university topper (2014-17)

With stark interest in the field of interventions, he then pursued a fellowship in pulmonology from CMC, Vellore (2017-19).

He also has also cleared HERMES in 2019 and is a European Diplomat in Adult Respiratory Medicine (EDARM)

Dr. Sameer is a fellow of Asian Pacific Society of Respirology (FAPSR), member of national academy of medical sciences (MNAMS).

He is a certified tobacco cessation expert (recognized course of Gujarat university)

He has worked as a consultant pulmonologist in CMC, Vellore. He was a consultant in critical care and pulmonology in Ozone Hospital, Akola and has played a vital role in treating thousands of covid patients during the pandemic. He was also associated with Sir HN Hospital, Mumbai as a pulmonologist before he moved to Nagpur. He is currently a consultant interventional pulmonologist at Kingsway hospital, Nagpur and Director and Consultant at Panacea clinic, Dhantoli, Nagpur.

Research and publications has been his forte and application of research to clinical practice is what he strives for to provide patients with latest state-of-art treatment. Dr. Sameer was an eminent faculty in formation of National guidelines for pleural effusion. He was an invited faculty in a workshop held in Napcon 2022 at Varanasi.

Being an academic scholar since the beginning, he has multiple research publications to his cap.

  • Co-author in Lessons learnt from the Indian H1N1 (swine flu) epidemic: Predictors of outcome based on epidemiological and clinical profile. (J Family Med Prim Care. 2018 Nov-Dec; 7(6): 1506–1509. doi: 10.4103/jfmpc.jfmpc_38_18 )
  • Presented 3 posters (co-author) and 2 papers (co-author) at national level conference- Napcon 2017.
  • MD Thesis- Comparison of clinical, radiological and microbiological profile in smokers and nonsmokers suffering from new sputum positive pulmonary tuberculosis.
  • Presented Poster (Prediction equation for PFT in Bengali subjects) at national level conference Napcon 2019.

    • Having exceptional oratory skills and great command over 6 languages (english, hindi, Marathi, gujarati, tamil, bengali) he is able to connect extremely well with his patients.

    His motto has always been “patient first” and strives hard to provide excellent care using all his knowledge and skills.
