Dr. Arundhati Marathe Lote

MBBS, MS, Fellowship in Endocrine Surgery from CMC, Vellore,
Clinical research fellowship in Breast Surgical O ncology from Tata Memorial Hospital, Mumbai

Dr. Arundhati Marathe Lote is a well-known and prominent name in Central India. Her expertise lies in treatment of all benign and malignant conditions of breast, thyroid and parathyroid surgeries. She has performed more than 500 various breast and axillary surgeries including breast conservation procedures and has a very skillful approach to thyroid and parathyroid surgical disorders.

Dr. Arundhati has meritoriously completed her MBBS from Vikhe Patil Medical College (VIMS), Ahmednagar and was always amongst the top 10 rankers in II/III (part I-II) MBBS(2007-12). Post MBBS, she went on to do her internship in KJ Somaiya Medical College, Mumbai with good hands-on training and exposure in surgical departments(2012-13). Since her MBBS days, she was focused on surgical branches and her interest was quite prominent from her score in subjects like anatomy and general surgery.

She has perused her dream branch of MS in General surgery from Pramukhswami Medical college and Shri Krishna Hospital, Anand, Gujarat (2015-18) where she was a second ranker in her batch

In those 3 years of residency, Dr. Arundhati realized the social stigma a woman faces when she has issues pertaining to her intimate health and hygiene, let alone getting examined from a doctor of other gender. This is one of the biggest contributory factors for increased incidence of locally advanced and metastatic breast cancer cases in India. A lady surgeon is always the one who can comfort a patient and understand all her social dilemmas. Keeping this as her motto, to treat women with breast cancer, she then specialized in endocrine and breast surgery from prestigious institutes in India. She has a fellowship in endocrine surgery from CMC, Vellore(2018-19) and a Clinical research fellowship in breast surgical oncology from Tata Memorial Hospital, Mumbai (2020-21)

She has a research oriented aptitude and has a bagful of presentations and research work to her credit.

  • Poster presentation on hypothenar hammer syndrome in Gujsurgicon at Ahmedabad 2016.
